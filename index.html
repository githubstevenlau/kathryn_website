About Kathryn


Managing Knowledge Lawyer

Legal 500-recognised knowledge professional with over 20 years' corporate and personal immigration experience gained in the legal services and legal publishing industries. Expert in analysing complex immigration laws and their practical application. Public speaker, commentator and public policy influencer.

I hold a Bachelor of Economics / Bachelor of Law awarded by Macquarie University, Sydney, Australia and am qualified as a legal practitioner in New South Wales, Australia. I have over two decades of experience working in the UK immigration advice sector, including in global and boutique firms and in developing an immigration practical guidance product for the legal publisher, LexisNexis.

I am a member of the Immigration Law Practitioners' Association and serve on the Consulting Editorial Board for Lexis+ Practical Guidance Immigration. I also regularly contribute to law and policy clarification and development through the Home Office's stakeholder engagement activities.
